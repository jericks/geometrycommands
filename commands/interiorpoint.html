Name:

geom interiorpoint

Description:

Calculate the interior point of the input geometry.

Arguments:

  • -g –geometry: The input geometry
  • –help : Print help message

Example:

geom interiorpoint -g "POLYGON ((8 54, 8 51, 14 51, 14 47, 7 47, 7 42, 17 45, 16 53, 13 53, 8 54))"

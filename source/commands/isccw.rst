isccw
=====

**Name**:

geom isccw

**Description**:

Determine if the input geometry's coordinates are oriented counter clockwise or not 

**Arguments**:

   * -g --geometry: The input geometry

   * --help : Print help message



**Example**::

    geom isccw -g "LINEARRING (15 20, 10 20, 10 10, 15 10, 15 20)"
